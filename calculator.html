Calc.js

  • Most of the basic math operations and expressions are supported:
    • Powers  ^
    • Square Roots  √
    • Multiplication *
    • Division  /
    • Addition  +
    • Subtraction  -
  • Parentheses have to be followed by a mathematic operator
    • e.g.: (2+1)*(6-3) and not (2+1)(6-3)
  • Nested parentheses for additional operation priority are supported
    • e.g.: (1+3-(2-6*(4-3)+3)*2)+1
  • Square root operator only accepts positive numbers
  • Input can be given from a physical keyboard or the on-screen keypad
    • An additional row of buttons for parentheses and exponentation in the nth degree
      is available by pressing the expand button on the on-screen keypad
